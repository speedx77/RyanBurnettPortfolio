Background

Ryan Burnett is a Brown University '22 graduate, currently employed at Paramount+ as a Senior Production Coordinator and QA Lead.

In the Spring of 2025, Ryan will attend Georgia Tech part time to obtain his Masters in Computer Science through GA Tech's online program.
